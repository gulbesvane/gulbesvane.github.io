People

For me, the smiles, the emotions and memories, created through the experience, are the main motivation behind anything that I do. I believe that a well-designed experience can be a life-changing event and can have a positive impact on a person's life.

Technology

I see technology as a tool that offers a plethora of opportunities to achieve aspirations and dreams, and also solve issues and obstacles, rooted in the experiences of humankind. I want to use technology to make every day lives better for people but also to create innovative, joyous and magical experiences.

Experiences

Experiences are so intangible and unique, and that's exactly what makes them so interesting and challenging to explore and design but when you get it right the rewards are remarkable!
